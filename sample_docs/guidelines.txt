
    POC Evaluation Guidelines
    ========================
    
    Health Status Definitions:
    - Healthy: All milestones on track, Winscore > 70
    - Warning: Some delays, Winscore 40-70
    - Critical: Major blockers, Winscore < 40
    
    Required Fields:
    - Deal Name
    - Account Executive
    - POC Duration (weeks)
    - Success Criteria
    - Current Status
